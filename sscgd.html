SSC GD Exam - Detailed Overview

The Staff Selection Commission - General Duty (SSC GD) exam is a national-level exam to recruit candidates in various paramilitary and police forces such as BSF, CISF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB, etc. It tests candidates on General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Knowledge, Elementary Mathematics, and English/Hindi.

After the written exam, candidates face physical tests including running, long jump, and high jump. Minimum physical standards vary by category. Preparation includes practice of reasoning, current affairs, mathematics, and maintaining fitness.

This exam offers a secure government job with good pay and benefits. Regular practice and dedication are key for success.
