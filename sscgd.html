About SSC GD Exam

The SSC General Duty (GD) exam is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission for recruitment of candidates in various paramilitary forces, police departments, and other allied services across India. It is one of the most sought-after exams for candidates aspiring to serve the nation in uniformed roles.

The exam assesses candidates on multiple aspects including physical fitness, reasoning skills, general awareness, and quantitative aptitude. Candidates are expected to have a sound knowledge of current events, basic mathematics, and the ability to solve problems quickly and accurately.

Selection through SSC GD opens career paths in forces like Border Security Force (BSF), Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF), Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP), and others. The exam tests both mental and physical preparedness, making it crucial for aspirants to maintain a disciplined study routine.

Candidates are advised to practice previous year papers, stay updated on national and international events, and improve their physical stamina to succeed.

The SSC GD exam process includes written examination, physical efficiency test (PET), physical standard test (PST), and document verification. Proper preparation and understanding of syllabus are key to cracking this exam.
