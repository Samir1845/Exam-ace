About SSC CGL

The Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL) examination is one of the most popular and competitive exams in India for graduate candidates seeking government jobs in ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India. The exam tests candidates on various skills including General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude, English Language, and General Awareness.

SSC CGL is conducted annually and consists of multiple tiers ‚Äì Tier 1 (Preliminary), Tier 2 (Mains), Tier 3 (Descriptive), and Tier 4 (Skill Test/Computer Proficiency Test). Each tier tests different capabilities from reasoning skills, quantitative skills to writing and computer proficiency.

Candidates preparing for SSC CGL should have a strong grasp of basics in arithmetic, reasoning, English grammar, and current affairs to score well. The exam offers a gateway to coveted government posts such as Inspectors, Assistants, Auditors, and more.

The selection process is highly competitive, requiring dedicated preparation and consistent practice. This page provides detailed subjects and custom questions to help aspirants practice and prepare effectively.
